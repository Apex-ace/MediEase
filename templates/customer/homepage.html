How to Use MediEase

Login & Register
  1. Click the "Login" button in the top navigation bar
  2. Enter your username and password
  3. New user? Click "Sign Up" to create an account
  4. After login, you'll have access to all features
Shop & Order
  1. Click "Shop" in the navigation menu
  2. Use the search bar to find specific medicines
  3. Add items to your cart by clicking "Add to Cart"
  4. Go to "Cart" and click "Checkout" to complete your order
Manage Orders
  1. Click "My Account" in the navigation menu
  2. View your order history in the "My Orders" tab
  3. Click "View Details" to see complete order information
  4. Cancel pending orders using the "Cancel Order" button
